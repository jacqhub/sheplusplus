Who Are We?

We are the ACM-Women's Chapter: SHE++.

ACM-W supports, celebrates, and advocates internationally for the full engagement of women in all aspects of the computing field,
providing a wide range of programs and services to ACM members and working in the larger community to advance the
contributions of technical women.
